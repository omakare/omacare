About Hypophosphatasia (HPP)

Hypophosphatasia is a rare genetic disorder affecting bone and tooth mineralization. Omacare is dedicated to providing support, information, and resources for individuals and families affected by HPP.

HPP Symptoms

  • Soft, weakened bones (rickets or osteomalacia)
  • Early loss of baby teeth
  • Repeated fractures
  • Muscle weakness
  • Joint pain and inflammation
  • Respiratory problems (in severe cases)

Available Treatments

  • Enzyme replacement therapy (asfotase alfa)
  • Supportive treatments for symptoms
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • Dental care and management

Patient Organizations

  • Soft Bones Foundation
  • Hypophosphatasia Support & Information Network
  • National Organization for Rare Disorders (NORD)

Our Services

  • Educational resources about HPP

  • Support group facilitation
  • Assistance with treatment access
